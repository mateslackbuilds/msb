This is a list of dependencies for the packages in the /extra directory.

1.  extra/mate-applets requires deps/libgtop and deps/gtksourceview

2.  extra/caja-extensions requires deps/gssdp and deps/gupnp

3.  extra/mate-utils requires deps/libgtop

4.  extra/pluma requires both deps/gtksourceview and deps/pygtksourceview

5.  extra/caja-dropbox requires deps/doc-utils

6.  extra/atril requires deps/libgxps
